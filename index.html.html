Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Crafted with Love

Founded in 2010 by Chef Ramesh Iyer, Spice Garden was born from a simple dream — to share the rich tapestry of Indian regional cuisine with Bangalore. Every dish is a journey through the subcontinent's diverse culinary heritage.

We source our spices directly from farmers in Kerala, Rajasthan and Kashmir — hand-ground fresh each morning. No shortcuts, no compromises.
